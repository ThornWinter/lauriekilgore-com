A Bit About Me...

Painting is my passion. It is what drives and motivates me. My love for Art started at a young age, but I didn’t fully pursue my Art until my early 20’s. I have been painting and creating works of Art for the past fifteen years since then.

I grew up in Monticello, NY, and have been an active artist, painter, and art educator in my beloved Sullivan County for all of my life. I graduated with a B.S. in Art Education and a Masters in Studio Art, with a focus in Painting, from SUNY New Paltz. I have participated in several group exhibitions including my Thesis Show Ten, in Poughkeepsie, New York, Roark’s Tavern, in Monticello, New York, and the Basha Kill Vineyard in Wurtsboro, New York.

This past year I displayed my work at group shows through the Catskill Art Society and Tivolli Artists' Gallery. I have created Public Art Murals in Hurleyville, Monticello, and Rock Hill, New York and have painted several murals, signs, and outdoor paintings for Sullivan Renaissance Projects. Those projects include outdoor murals on the main street of Rock Hill, New York, paintings of Roark’s Tavern in Monticello, a series of signs and outdoor murals for the Town of Thompson Park, and historical murals at the Sullivan County Historical Museum in Hurleyville.

I currently teach Art at the Monticello Central School District and Nesin Cultural Arts and I create original Art in my studio-business, Kilgore Artistry in Monticello, New York.
